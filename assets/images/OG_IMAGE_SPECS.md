## Design Guidelines

### Brand Colors
- **Primary**: #1e3a5f (Deep Blue - Trust/Authority)
- **Secondary**: #059669 (Emerald - Zambia/Sovereignty)  
- **Copper**: #DE6E00 (Heritage/Innovation)
- **Text**: #1a1a1a or white (depending on background)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Charter or serif font (authority, gravitas)
- **Body/Stats**: Inter or sans-serif (readability)
- **Weights**: Bold (700) for numbers, Semi-bold (600) for labels

### Image Quality
- **Format**: PNG (for transparency and quality)
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI minimum, 150 DPI preferred
- **File Size**: <300KB for fast loading
- **Compression**: Use PNG optimization tools
