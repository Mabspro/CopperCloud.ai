### **Title**: "The Drought Experiment: When the Grid Failed, Two Economies Diverged"

**Script**:
"The 2024 El Niño drought was a natural experiment. Kariba Dam nearly shut down. Load shedding hit 21 hours per day.

What happened?

**Tier 1 (Mining Sector)**:
- Private utility (CEC) imported power from Southern Africa
- Mining production INCREASED: Copper +12%, Cobalt +581%
- First Quantum: 'Normal operations continued'

**Tier 2 (Domestic Economy)**:
- Manufacturing paralyzed (couldn't afford diesel generators)
- Maize harvest collapsed 50%
- 5.8 million people faced food insecurity
- GDP growth revised from 4.5% to 1.2%

The lesson: Success in Zambia requires bypassing the national system entirely."

**Key Stat**:
- **Mining GDP**: Grew despite crisis
- **Domestic GDP**: Collapsed
- **Power deficit**: 1,381 MW (vs. 2,400 MW demand)
