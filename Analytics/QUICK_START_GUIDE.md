### 2. **zambia_3d_infrastructure_map.html**
**Purpose**: 3D elevation view of economic concentration
**What it shows**:
- Height of markers = Economic activity (taller = more activity)
- Color = Electrification rate (red = high, blue = low)
- Size = Infrastructure quality
- Can rotate 360¬∞ to see from any angle

**Use case**:
- "Wow factor" for presentations
- Demonstrating the VERTICAL disparity (Line of Rail towers above neglected areas)
- Shows the "mountain range" of concentrated development

**Key Insight**: The vertical dimension makes the inequality impossible to miss. Lusaka and the Copperbelt are literal economic peaks while neglected provinces are flat plains.

**Pro tip**: In presentation mode, rotate it slowly to show the dramatic height differences from different angles.

### 3. **zambia_network_grid.html**
**Purpose**: Infrastructure network connectivity visualization
**What it shows**:
- Lines = Transport corridors (rail, road)
- Red lines = Established rail (Line of Rail)
- Green lines = Planned corridors (Lobito)
- Gray lines = Road connections
- Line thickness = Capacity
- Nodes = Cities (size by economic activity)

**Use case**:
- Explaining infrastructure gaps
- Showing how the "grid" is actually just a spine, not a grid
- Demonstrating corridor development strategy

**Key Insight**: The visualization reveals that what's called a "national grid" is really just a few connected lines - not a grid at all.

**Strategic application**: "This is where capital flows TODAY. But look at these green dotted lines - that's where it will flow TOMORROW with the Lobito Corridor."

### 5. **zambia_opportunity_heatmap.html**
**Purpose**: Investment opportunity matrix by province
**What it shows**:
- Heatmap rows:
  1. Agricultural Potential (inherent capacity)

  3. Infrastructure Gap (percentage lacking)

- Green = High opportunity
- Yellow = Moderate
- Red = Low/already developed

**Use case**:
- Portfolio construction decisions
- Prioritizing target provinces
- Fund X market entry targeting
- ZECS platform data visualization

**Key Insight**: Muchinga, Luapula, Northern are bright green across all metrics - highest ROI targets.

**Strategic application**: "If we overlay this heatmap with infrastructure arrival timing, we get our investment sequencing."
